## About Mythic WoW

Here at Mythic WoW we are a small team of dedicated developers, determined to bring back the classic feeling with new custom gamemodes including Hardcore/Semi-Hardcore Ironman and more, our current main focus is on The Burnign Crusade but we will move to other classic xpacs in due time.

Our project is based off of Cmangos: a MMORPG Framework based mostly in C++.
Our core is private sourced.
https://MythicWoW
